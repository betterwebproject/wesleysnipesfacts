Wesley Snipes FactsTM

FAQ

1. Was Wesley Snipes a midget?

Yes, according to post-mortem medical reports, Wesley Snipes was a pituitary midget.

2. How tall was Wesley Snipes?

Reports vary, but it is believed that Snipes stood between 3’7” and 3’10” without his elevator shoes.

3. Is Wesley Snipes still in prison?

No, he isn’t. Wesley Snipes was released from FCI McKean in 2011, a year before he died.

4. Did Wesley Snipes know any martial arts?

No, Snipes was proficient at parkour, but never studied martial arts.

5. How many black belts did Wesley Snipes have?

None.

6. Did Wesley Snipes invent Kwanzaa?

No, Wesley Snipes didn’t invent Kwanzaa, but when alive, often suggested that he had contributed to its renaissance in the U.S. in the 1990s.

7. Was Wesley Snipes related to Fiona Apple?

Yes, Snipes’ first cousin, Glendalia Pruner, was Fiona Apple’s biological mother.

8. Was Wesley Snipes missing fingers?

Yes, like many pituitary midgets, Snipes only had four fingers on each hand, despite a number of childhood surgeries.

9. Did Wesley Snipes speak Pig Latin fluently?

Yes, in addition to many other talents, Snipes was a respected Pig Latinist.
